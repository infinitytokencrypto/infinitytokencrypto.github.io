ABOUT

Infinity is a community-driven token built on the Binance Smart Chain that rewards $BUSD instead of tokens. Our mission is to develop a swapping platform with vetting features for ease of access when buying a coin, which allows you to know what you’re buying into with a safety rating, coins can also personally vet for better score, infinistaking is coming up where you will be able to stake your coins for rewards and more undisclosed utility’s are in the works for the future.

TOKENOMICS

2% BUSD 2% of every transaction is taken and redistributed to all $INFINITY holders. Hold $INFINITY tokens, earn $BUSD.
8% Marketing 8% of every transaction is taken and will be used for enhancements, operations, and marketing of the project.
1% Liquidity 1% of every transaction is transformed into liquidity. It's automatic and helps create a price floor.
